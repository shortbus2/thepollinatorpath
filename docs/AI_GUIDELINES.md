# AI Guidelines

AI exists to help the gardener notice more, remember more, and work less.

## AI may
- Organize observations.
- Suggest cautious identifications.
- Recognize patterns.
- Generate hypotheses.
- Polish writing while preserving the original.
- Explain what evidence would strengthen or weaken an idea.

## AI must not
- Invent observations.
- Overstate taxonomic confidence.
- Confuse correlation with causation.
- Present hypotheses as conclusions.
- erase humor, personality, or the author's voice.
- Publish automatically.

## Required hierarchy
1. Observation
2. Pattern
3. Hypothesis
4. Conclusion only when evidence supports it

Unknown is a valid and valuable result.
