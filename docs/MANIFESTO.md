# Manifesto

- Preserve joy.
- Curiosity is contagious.
- Privacy is designed in, not cleaned up later.
- Facts are facts. Hypotheses are hypotheses. Never confuse the two.
- Nature becomes more amazing as we learn more.
- Memories fade. Stories help us remember why moments mattered.
- Data supports the story. The story gives the data meaning.
- Every feature should help someone notice, understand, or wonder.
- The goal is not to admire one garden, but to inspire curiosity about another.
