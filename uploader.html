Local maintenance tool

The Pollinator Path Photo Uploader

This runs on your computer. It prepares web-sized JPGs, files them under the correct plant or visitor, and updates the gallery manifest. Nothing is uploaded anywhere until you commit and push with GitHub Desktop.

Drop photographs here
or click to choose JPG, PNG, WebP, HEIC, or HEIF files
Choose the cloned thepollinatorpath repository folder first.

What the tool does

HEIC/HEIF files are converted to JPG. Every image is resized to a maximum of 1600 pixels on its longest side and saved at web-friendly quality. Gallery files receive descriptive date-based names. The tool then rewrites image-manifest.js, which tells the website exactly which images belong on each page.

Use Microsoft Edge or Google Chrome on Windows. Keep your full-resolution originals in Apple Photos; these are optimized website copies.
