The very manageable starter plan

  1. Choose one small sunny or partly shaded area.
  2. Check how much water and sun it actually receives.
  3. Plant three compatible plants rather than one lonely decorative hostage.
  4. Leave the seed heads through winter.
  5. Watch who arrives.

Congratulations. You have habitat.
